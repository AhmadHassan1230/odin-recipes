Meatballs

Description

Learn how to make meatballs with this recipe that is the best meatball recipe I have ever tried! I've used the combination of beef, and veal but you can make them with just ground beef and they still taste great. Definitely use fresh bread crumbs and freshly grate your cheese instead of using the canned variety... It really does make a difference!

Ingredients

  • Ground meat
  • Seasoning and Herbs
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Italian Bread
  • Water
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Combine first 8 ingredients in a bowl
  2. Add the bread crumbs
  3. Add water
  4. Shape the mixture into meatballs
  5. Fry the meatballs until they're all brown and crisp

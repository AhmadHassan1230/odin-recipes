Margherita Pizza

Description

This is a fantastic version of an Italian classic. The feta cheese adds a rich flavor that brings this dish to life. Incredibly easy and incredibly delicious!

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Minced Garlic
  • Sea Salt
  • Sliced Roma Tomatoes
  • Pre Baked Pizza Crusts
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Fontina Cheese
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • Crumbled Feta Cheese

Steps

  1. Stir together olive oil, garlic, and salt; toss with tomatoes, and leave for 15 minutes
  2. Brush pizza crust with tomato marinade and sprink;e mozzarella and fontina cheese.
  3. Arrange tomatoes overtop, then sprinkle shredded basil, Parmesan, and feta cheese
  4. Bake in preheated oven until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown
